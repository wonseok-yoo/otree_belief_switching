Experiment 1 consists of 4 scenarios with 3 problems each. In total, you will be solving 12 problems.

At the end of the experiment, the computer will randomly pick one of the 3 problems for payment for each scenario.

In other words, the computer will pick 4 problems, one problem for one scenario.

For each problem, you will be asked to complete tasks.

If the problem was selected for payment, the computer will randomly pick one of the decisions you made in the problem to determine your payment.
