You will be asked to do a similar task as in Scenario 0.

Except that

- The box is now yours and your payment depends on which box you have.

- You will be able to swap the box if you want at the end of the 5th round.

You will be presented with two boxes: the Green Box and the Red Box.(As shown in the image below)

- The Green Box contains 5 green balls and 4 red balls.

- The Red Box has 5 red balls and 4 green balls.

The computer will randomly select one of the two boxes. You will not be informed which box has been chosen, whether it is the Green Box or the Red Box.

Your task is to guess the probability that the box is the Green Box and to choose either to swap or keep the box.

You will be asked to tell us your best guess that the chosen box is the Green Box.

Next, we will randomly draw a ball from the selected box and show it to you. Then, you will be asked to report the likelihood that you think the chosen box is the Green Box.

You will draw balls for 5 rounds. After each round, you will be asked to tell us your best guess.

After the 5th round, if you would like, you will be able to swap the box without any cost.

The left is the Green Box and the right is the Red Box.

Click "Next" to proceed to the experiment.
