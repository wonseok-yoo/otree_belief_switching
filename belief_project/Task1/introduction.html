There are two boxes, Box 1 and Box 2.

Box 1 contains 5 green balls and 4 red balls while Box 2 has 4 green balls and 5 red balls.

You will be endowed with one of the two boxes with probability 1/2.

Then you will draw a ball with a replacement from the box you have for 5 rounds.

After each round, you will be asked to report the belief that you think the box you have is Box 1.

After 5 rounds, you will be able to keep the box or to swap the box.

If you end up having Box 1, you get $1.5, otherwise you get $0.5.

Click "Next" to proceed to the experiment.
